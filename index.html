family

My family has always pushed me to follow my passion and be the best I can be. My late grandfather has always been my greatest role model; he supported my art and taught me the importance of listening to your intuition. I chose to attend UCF in order to stay close to my family. I am also a first generation student, and will be the first person in my family to earn a degree.

art

At a very young age, I discovered my creativity. I would draw and paint everyday. I never thought it would lead me to my career but after examining my passions and choices, I realized Web Design would allow me to take that creativity and combine it with my other strenghts. By applying my natural talents and expanding my knowledge, I believe this career path is perfect for me.

graphic design

During my time at Seminole State College, I took some graphic design courses that introduced me to the world of digital media. I realized that I love both coding and design and wanted to combine the two to find a career that suits me best. My goal is to be a front end developer but also capable of designing the brand and website myself. My favorite type of design is that that focuses on natural elements (such as organic shapes and materials.)

travel

At the age of 17, I moved across the Atlantic on my own to live in the Netherlands as I explored Europe. I realized one of the best feelings, to me, is exploring a new place and meeting new people. Cities, small towns, mountains, or beaches; I love exploring the world around me. When I learned there was a way to combine travel and work by becoming a digital nomad, I realized that was what I wanted in life.
